## Study Design

164 paramedics

Two groups, three intubation attempts:

+ AAB: 1 -- 2 -- **Training** -- 3
+ ABB: 1 -- **Training** -- 2 -- 3


---

{{< slide background-color="#BCE78A" >}}
## Primary Outcome

Proportion of successful intubations between groups on the second intubation attempt
